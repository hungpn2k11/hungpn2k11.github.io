How to Use This App

  1. Login or Sign Up with your email and password.
  2. Click “Add Entry” to record a volunteer activity.
  3. Enter organization name, supervisor, date, time, and a short description of what you did.
  4. Your total hours will automatically update at the top.
  5. You can use “Download PDF” or “Download CSV” to optionally export a copy of your hours.
  6. Click “Submit to School” to open the RHS form pre-filled with your entry.
  7. Use the Goal section to track progress toward your required hours.
  8. Use Profile to set your name, grade, and school (this info is used for PDF exports!).
  9. Tip: Use the "Save current as template" button in the form to quickly fill in info for organizations you visit often.
